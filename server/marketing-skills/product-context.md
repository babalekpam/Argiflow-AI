## What ArgiFlow Does

ArgiFlow is an AI-powered lead generation and email outreach platform that helps businesses automate their B2B sales outreach from prospecting to close. It combines lead scraping, email sequence building, AI personalization, CRM, and a smart AI sales assistant (Aria) into one platform.

**Core features:**
- AI-powered cold email sequence builder (multi-touch, personalized)
- B2B lead scraper (12+ free data sources, zero-cost)
- Aria — AI floating chat widget and sales rep powered by Claude
- CRM with pipeline tracking
- Email delivery infrastructure (Amazon SES relay)
- White-label domain onboarding
- LinkedIn + forum prospecting
- Landing page builder
- Social media management
- Calendar & automations
- Invoicing module
